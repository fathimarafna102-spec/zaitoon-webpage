About Our Campus

Step into Zaitoon International Girls Campus at Cheemeni, Payyannur, where education and morality come together to shape the future of young women.

As an exclusive residential campus for girls, we combine world-class academics, NEET & JEE coaching, leadership development, and Islamic studies to create confident, responsible, and successful students.

Our mission is to nurture every student with knowledge, discipline, compassion, and strong moral values while preparing them for future academic and life challenges.

Admission Guidelines

Required Documents

ZSE Admission Guidelines

  1. Visit the campus and collect the admission form.
  2. Submit the completed application form..
  3. Carefully read and understand the fee details and affidavit before signing.
  4. Contact the transportation coordinator to confirm the availability of transport service in your area.
  5. Confirm your admission by paying the amount to the given account. This amount will be adjusted as your first-term fee.
